About Me

Creating useful and user-centered applications is something I really enjoy doing, and alongside that, I am developing my skills in iOS app development and web development. I am a member of the web/app development department of the iSpace Club at my college. In this position, I work with other developers, help out in the student tech initiatives, and constantly work on getting my technical skills up to par through project-based learning.

Xcode is my primary development environment, and I am developing iOS applications using both Swift and SwiftUI. At the moment I have two apps under development which are targeted towards improving the productivity of the users. The first one is Notion Widgets, an app for iOS that allows the user to connect their Notion account in a secure way and also shows the database information directly on the user's home screen widgets. The second one is a Student Planner which aims to assist the students in organizing their schedules, classes, and calendar events along with managing to-dos, and getting course details while tracking classes through Live Activities. Both the applications are yet to be completed, but they have made me gain better understanding in app architecture, APIs, data handling and designing clean, user-oriented UI.

Besides working on mobile development, I have also been involved in full-stack web development projects. When I interned at Geo Pacific Solutions, I was a part of a team of six, who developed a web-based system for centralized billing and stock management similar to Tally, which would permit the businesses to track the inventory, control billing and the whole process of invoicing. This exposure not only helped me to understand the real-world requirement but also gave me an experience of being part of a team, practicing version control, and learning the basics of large-scale system design.

I like to tackle challenges, get familiar with new technologies, and create innovative and convenient solutions for daily problems. My aim is to gradually upskill in both app and web development, while still being involved in projects that make a difference.
